About the employer

Datalogics

Internet & Software

50-100 employees

Wilmington, DE

Datalogics was founded in 1967 in Chicago, IL. We developed one of the first computerized typesetting systems, and the editing workstations to drive them. In the early 1980s, we took part in the ISO committee to standardize SGML (the forerunners of XML and HTML). We applied this knowledge when developing DL Pager and DL Composer publishing systems. Datalogics was purchased by Frame Technology Corporation in 1983, which was then purchased by Adobe Systems. We were then spun off as a privately-held independent company. Adobe retained partial interest as an Adobe Ventures Company. Since 1997, weve worked closely with Adobe, acting as the key channel for several of their PDF toolkits. These include Adobe PDF Library, Adobe PDF Print Engine, Adobe InDesign Server, and the Adobe Reader Mobile SDK. Today, our business focuses on providing advanced technologies to software developers. Weve enabled them to bring their products to market faster with the best PDF capabilities. We support hundreds of customers worldwide who use our technology in mission-critical applications. Technology has changed dramatically over the past 50 years, but our core values remain. We remain committed to supporting our customers and providing them with the best possible products and services. In 2023 Datalogics transitioned to a fully remote workforce with employees in 12 states.
